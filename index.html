Backprop Golf

Learn Backpropagation Through Mini Golf

Choose an angle and force to hit the ball. The ball bounces around following physics. After each shot, backpropagation computes the gradient of the loss (distance² to hole) with respect to your parameters. Use the gradient to make small adjustments — this is gradient descent!
